Privacy policy

This service is not launched yet. We don't track you.

CertSleuth stores the account details you provide, the certifications and CEU activities you add, and links you supply to evidence hosted elsewhere. We do not store evidence documents.

If you use inbox scanning, we perform a one-time, read-only search of your Gmail for certification emails from known issuers, keep only the extracted certification details, and discard the access token and message contents immediately. We never store email bodies and never access your inbox in the background.

We use no third-party analytics or advertising. Transactional email is sent through our email provider. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time; deletion is immediate and complete.
